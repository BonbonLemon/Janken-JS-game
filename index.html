Janken (Ro Sham Bo)

Press "Space" to start

Move: Left/Right Arrows Space: Fire! Instructions: Several cards with either Rock, Paper, or Scissors hand gestures on them fly around the top screen. At the bottom of the screen are 3 cards, also with Rock/Paper/Scissors hand gestures on them. You earn points by shooting the hands at the bottom into the hands spinning around. You have a time limit and only 5 hands. The rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors apply: -Winning removes the card and rewards you 1 points. -Ties removes the card but rewards you no points. -Losing does not remove the card and you lose a life. -Missing the cards completely does nothing. If you remove all the cards, more will appear and you are given 10 extra seconds.
